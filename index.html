Everything ink should do on a screen

Four things HandyNotes takes seriously, so you don't have to think about them.

Ink engine

Ink that keeps pace with your hand

A custom Metal GPU engine renders every stroke the instant you make it — tuned for 120 Hz ProMotion and ~240 Hz Apple Pencil sampling, with full pressure, tilt, hover, and Pencil Pro squeeze.

  • 120 Hz ProMotion
  • ~240 Hz sampling
  • Pressure · tilt · hover
  • Pencil Pro squeeze

Search

Search your own handwriting

Every word is indexed on-device the moment you write it, using ML Kit Digital Ink recognition with Vision OCR as a fallback. Find anything you've ever written, without leaving your notebooks.

  • English
  • Russian
  • Ukrainian
  • Spanish
  • French
  • German
  • Italian
  • Portuguese

PDF import

Turn any PDF into editable ink

Import a PDF and HandyNotes converts it into real, erasable pen strokes — automatically detecting paper ruling so lined and grid templates come through intact. Export back out as a true vector PDF whenever you're done.

  • Editable vector strokes
  • Auto paper-ruling detection
  • True-vector PDF export

For students

Built for the way students actually study

Geometry snapping straightens freehand lines, circles, arcs, and curves with a draggable ruler. Organize work in folders, tags, and multi-page notebooks, keep a source open in a floating reference window, and write in English or Russian — fully offline.

  • Geometry snapping
  • Folders · tags · notebooks
  • Floating reference window
  • Offline · free
